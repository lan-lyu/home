About Me

I was born and grew up in Hangzhou, Zhejiang, East China. It is a city with long history and beautiful scenary. It is a city that develop at a ever fast pace. Sometimes I feel we grow at the same speed.

Family means a lot to me. Since middle school, I started boarding at school and went home once a week. My parents and I would spend every weekend on parties, mountain climbing, or a well-prepared meal for families. Every vacation I stayed at my grandparent's house - villages hidden deep in the mountains. I love to lie down and see the stars shifting from one side in the sky to the other side. You can never see that in a city.

I love to visit different places and explore diverse cultures. I have been to 20+ provinces in China, also other countries like Thailand, Korea, Japan, and United States. I love spending time with natives, talking to them and hearing their personal stories, also take great photos with memory.
